Monitor Instructions

Your Role

As a monitor, you help locate students throughout the school when parents request pickup.

Steps to Follow

  1. Check this screen regularly for new pickup requests
  2. Locate the student using their photo and class information
  3. Escort the student to the pickup area
  4. Mark the student as "Found" in the system
  5. Inform security that the student is ready for pickup
